## Who They Are

Jordi Waves blends organic instrumentation with electronic production in a way that feels like Tycho meets Khruangbin. Originally from Barcelona and now based in Berlin, he layers live guitar, bass, and found sounds over meticulously crafted beats. Every track sounds like a place you've been but can't quite name.

## Before The Signal

Jordi learned guitar from YouTube videos in a cramped dorm room in Barcelona, recording straight into his laptop's built-in mic. He couldn't afford monitors or decent headphones, so he mixed everything through earbuds. When he moved to Berlin on a one-way ticket with 400 euros in his pocket, he spent his first three months sleeping on friends' couches and playing open mics at bars that paid in beer. He got his break when a sound engineer at a warehouse party heard him playing guitar over a DJ set and asked him to collaborate. That connection eventually led to the Bonobo session that changed everything.
