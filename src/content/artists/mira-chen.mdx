## Who They Are

Mira Chen writes R&B that feels like reading someone's journal at 2 AM — confessional, unpolished, and disarmingly honest. Based in Oakland, she pairs lo-fi production with a voice that can shift from a whisper to something that fills an entire room. Her lyrics are the kind that make you pause and rewind.

## Before The Signal

Mira grew up singing in her church choir in Oakland but never considered music as a career until the pandemic shut everything down. Stuck at home with nothing but a phone and too many feelings, she started recording voice memos — raw, one-take captures of songs she wrote on her bedroom floor. She posted a cover of Frank Ocean's "Self Control" on TikTok almost as an afterthought. She woke up to 4.2 million views and 200 DMs from strangers telling her she made them cry. She still doesn't own a proper microphone — she records everything on a Shure SM58 she bought used for $60.
